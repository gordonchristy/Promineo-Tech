ACCOMODATING OUR STUDENTS

Our department is growing rapidly! We are proud of the fact that over the last year we our freshman class has more than doubled. We now have approximately 1,976 undergraduate students. Though we are grateful for the increase, it is impossible for our four Faculty Advisors to accommodate all of our students before registration begins in January. We have adopted a group advising model so that our advisors can advise everyone and clear advising pins before the registration.
