WHAT IS AN ACADEMIC ADVISOR?

An academic advisor serves as a counselor for college students who gives them advice concerning their major or minor programs of undergraduate/graduate study. An academic advisor also helps students register for classes and make sure they are meeting the requirements of their degree program. If a student is struggling with a particular course, the advisor may be able to refer the student to other resources on campus that specialize in student success. Advisors can also provide information on possible career paths that are available to students upon completion of their degree as well as provide information on graduate program requirements.

WHY IS ACADEMIC ADVISING IMPORTANT?

  • Ensure student registers for classes related to their degree program
  • To address academic issues early before student GPA is affected
  • To discuss strategies for student success
  • To assist students with change of major where needed
  • To assist help student become familiar with the registration process
